Simple nodejs API

Available url:

  • http://localhost:3000/api/
  • http://localhost:3000/api/vehicles
  • http://localhost:3000/api/vehicle/:vehicle_id -- Replace :vehicle_id with car ID, Example: 596cb18d4480820a006290df
  • http://localhost:3000/api/vehicle/make/:make -- Replace :make with car manufactor, Example: Ferrari
  • http://localhost:3000/api/vehicle/model/:model -- Replace :model with car model, Example: California
  • http://localhost:3000/api/vehicle/color/:color -- Replace :color with car color, Example: Red

Get Data from Mongo DB
